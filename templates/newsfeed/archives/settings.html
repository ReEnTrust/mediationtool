Settings

On this page, each algorithm is explained, then you can chose settings and observe the impact on the results.


What does Algorithm A compute?

This algorithm generates news content to Alex based on browsing history. The algorithm uses the cookies accepted by Alex to detect Alex’s visited pages.


What does Algorithm B compute?

This algorithm generates news content to Alex based on a similar user’s favourites. The algorithm requires Alex to create an account and share personal information.


What does Algorithm C compute?

This algorithm generates news content to Alex based on Alex’s current location. The algorithm shows local news to all users without any personalisation (no cookies, no account).


What does Algorithm D compute?

This algorithm shows sponsorised and popular news items to Alex (e.g . NHS official communication and Covid-19 news). The algorithm overrides Alex’s cookies, personal information and location. The news might not be adapted to Alex’s taste.
