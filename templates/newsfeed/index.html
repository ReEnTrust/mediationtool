Welcome to the Mediation Tool !

by ReEnTrust Project https://reentrust.org/

This tool is designed to investigate users' concerns about the algorithms of a newsfeed platform and the impact of a mediation process on such concerns.

The tool demonstrates how newsfeed algorithms work. As a user, you will express your opinion about some features of a newsfeed platform. You will also reflect on the presented mediation process and discuss how it impacts your opinion.

About

ReEnTrust is an EPSRC research project (EP/R033633/1) funded under the Trust, Identity, Privacy and Security in the Digital Economy 2.0 (TIPS2) call.

The project is a collaboration between researchers from:

  • Human Centred Computing Group at the, University of Oxford
  • The Centre for Intelligent Systems and their Applications (CISA) at the University of Edinburgh
  • HORIZON Digital Economy Research at the University of Nottingham
