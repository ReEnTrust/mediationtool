Scenario

Our fictionnal character

We present a hypothetical person browsing a newsfeed platform.

We’ll call the person Alex. Alex is 27 years old Web developer and designer, residing at Oxford, United Kingdom.

During the last few days, Alex has spent time reading news about International Politics. However, Alex usually likes and shares IT and Science related news on the platform.

Alex shares similar demographic information with another user Eli who usually likes and shares news about Entertainment and Arts.

Newsfeed platform algorithm

Newsfeed platforms use an algorithm to suggest the best news content to everyone. They may try to achieve this by the types of news a user read before (feature A), based on some broad demographic information about a user (feature B), by a user’s location (feature C), or prioritising sponsored news items (feature D).

To recommend news content to Alex, we present how the above four features can be used by a newsfeed algorithm. Each of these features are used to suggest to Alex what may matter the most to him.
