Make your own analysis of the algorithm

We present a hypothetical person browsing a newsfeed platform.

We’ll call the person Alex. Alex is 27 years old Web developer and designer, residing at Oxford, United Kingdom.

During the last few days, Alex has spent time reading news about International Politics. However, Alex usually likes and shares IT and Science related news on the platform.

Alex shares similar demographic information with another user Eli who usually likes and shares news about Entertainement and Arts.

A newsfeed platform aims to show their users the content that would fit the most with what they like and expect.

To recommend news items to Alex, the platform combines information it has collected about them. The algorithm is a mix of the four features that are explained just below.

The purpose of this page is to show you how each feature influences the news recommended to Alex and gives you the opportunity to give us your opinion on whether they are relevant.

Please read through each algorithm explanation and evaluate the suggested news items by giving your feedback at the bottom of the page.
